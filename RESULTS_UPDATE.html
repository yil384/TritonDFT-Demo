Key Findings

End-to-End Automation

TritonDFT successfully automates the complete DFT workflow, reducing manual intervention time by 80-90% based on our survey with 19 domain researchers.

Pareto Optimization

Pareto-aware parameter inference enables intelligent accuracy-cost trade-offs, with GPT 5.2 achieving 70.5% pass rate at the 20 meV/atom threshold.

Extensible Design

Support for 10 diverse material categories (metal, insulator, superconductor, topological materials, etc.) and >10 task types in general QE usage.

LLM Capability Analysis

Experiments reveal substantial capability differences: GPT 5.2 for accuracy, GPT 4o for cost efficiency, Claude Opus 4.5 for parallelization.
